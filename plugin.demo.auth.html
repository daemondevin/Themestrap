PluginAuth Live Demo

Every form below is enhanced by the real PluginAuth plugin running in your browser — inline validation, password show/hide, a strength meter, Caps Lock warning, busy state, and toast feedback. Submissions are intercepted here for the demo (no server), so try triggering the validation.

In production these forms are rendered by the tsLogin, tsRegister, tsForgotPassword, and tsProfile snippets and POST to the MODX Login extra. This page hard-codes the markup so you can preview the front-end behavior standalone.
