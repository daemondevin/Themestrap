FAQ

Common questions

Is the 14-day trial really free — no credit card needed?
Yes. You can trial any paid plan for 14 days without entering payment information. At the end of the trial you'll be asked to add a card if you want to continue. If you don't, you'll automatically move to the Free plan.
Can I change plans at any time?
Absolutely. Upgrade whenever you need more capacity — billing is prorated to the day. Downgrade takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
What happens if I go over my analytics event limit?
We'll send you a warning at 80% and 100% of your limit. We never drop events silently — instead we queue the overage and prompt you to upgrade. You can also set custom overage budgets on Growth and above.
Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?
Yes. We have a Startup Program (50% off Growth for 12 months) for early-stage companies backed by recognised accelerators, and a 40% discount for registered non-profits. Apply via the support portal.
Where is my data stored?
By default, data is stored in the US (AWS us-east-1). European customers can opt into EU-only storage (Germany, AWS eu-central-1) from the account settings panel. Enterprise customers can choose any supported region.
Can I export my data if I leave?
Yes, always. You can export your analytics events, deployment logs, and configuration as JSON or CSV at any time from the dashboard. We retain your data for 90 days after cancellation.
